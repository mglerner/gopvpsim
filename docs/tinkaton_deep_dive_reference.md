## tinkaton (gigaton hammer & bulldoze)

### attack

* 105.58 attack lets you win cmp tie against rank #1 corviknight (but this does not change any matchups)

* 105.79 attack lets you win cmp tie against default iv lickilicky which lets you win the 2s if neither of you bait

* 105.90 attack lets you win cmp tie against rank #1 (non best buddy) medicham (but this does not change any matchups)

* 105.90 attack lets you win cmp tie against rank #1 jellicent (but this does not change any matchups)

* 106.20 attack lets you beat jellicent in the 1-0s regardless of whether or not you shield a bait

* 106.58 attack gives a bulkpoint vs rank #1 annihilape (but this does not flip any matchups)

### bulk

* 140.21 defense gives a bulkpoint against attack-weighted shadow drapion (119.80 attack) which flips the 0-1s

* 140.91 defense gives a bulkpoint against rank #1 shadow politoed, which flips the 0s. this can also be done without the bulkpoint if you have 139 hp. 142.33 defense is needed for the bulkpoint against default iv shadow politoed. the bulkpoint and 139 hp are needed to tie the 2-1s (instead of losing)

* 141.66 defense with 138 hp lets you shield a dynamic punch & survive two more against the rank #1 medicham (non best buddy) and default iv medicham to win the 1s without baiting

* 143.03 defense gives a bulkpoint vs rank #1 azu which flips the 1-2s (no baiting required)

* 143.04 defense with 140hp lets you reach two gigaton hammers against high rank and default iv galarian corsola to win in the 0s, but this will not work against egg iv corsola. you can win this without the bulk by bulldozing first and getting the debuff to knock out with a gigaton hammer, but the bulk offers consistency

-* 143.04 defense with 141 hp (or 143.72 defense with 140 hp) lets you win the 0-1s against the rank #1 shadow altaria without baiting. this seems inconsistent, as shadow ivs vary & you no longer win against shadow altaria with a little bit of attack


### recommendation / summary

* the 0/14/9 and 0/15/8 tinkaton meet all of the bulk requirements above while having higher attack than default iv lickilicky

* given the high variance of galarian corsola ivs and shadow altaria & politoed ivs, a more reasonable goal would be to have 143.03 defense & 138 hp for the more consistent bulk benchmarks above. given that this only includes 13 iv spreads total, you may want to drop the high defense constraint from the azumarill bulkpoint and run a tinkaton with 141.66 defense & 138 hp and as much attack as possible for the important cmp ties. the 2/13/5 looks really solid, but all of the 85 iv spreads that meet these requirements look great
