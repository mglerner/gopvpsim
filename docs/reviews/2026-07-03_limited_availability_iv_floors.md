## Verified verdicts (3-0 votes unless noted)

### Melmetal: NOT limited -- default 12/12/12 treatment is correct (high)

The Mystery Box is repeatable indefinitely (3-day cooldown, re-armed by
sending any mon from GO to Let's Go or HOME -- free HOME tier suffices), and
Meltan appears as WILD encounters (floor 0/0/0, 4/4/4 weather-boosted).
Grindable, therefore not "stuck"; the 400-candy evolution itself presupposes
grinding. Primary source: Niantic Help Center (Mystery Box FAQ); Serebii +
Bulbapedia corroborate. If Melmetal was on anyone's candidate list for
`FLOOR_10_SPECIES`, take it off.

### New-species verdicts: nothing verifiably needs adding

- **Zacian (Crowned Sword) / Zamazenta (Crowned Shield)** (medium): the
  Crowned form is a TRANSFORMATION of a raid-caught Hero individual --
  Crowned IVs = the raid catch's IVs (raid floor 10). First change costs
  1,000 Crowned energy; after that, form swaps are free and unlimited
  (Niantic primary source). Crowned raids have already recurred (GO Fest 2025
  Global; Raid Hour Aug 2025), so re-obtainable when in rotation.
  Event-gated, not permanently stuck.
- **Necrozma (Dusk Mane / Dawn Wings)** (medium, 2-1 vote): fusion inherits
  BASE Necrozma's IVs exactly; Solgaleo/Lunala IVs are irrelevant. Fused
  floor = base Necrozma's floor (raids, recurring + GBL rank-20 pool). Side
  evidence (uncorroborated) reports the same inheritance rule for Kyurem
  Black/White fusions.
- **Solgaleo / Lunala** (medium): NOT capped at the one-time Cosmog research
  line -- both had 5-star raid debuts July 2025 and sat in the Season-23 GBL
  rank-20 reward pool. Re-obtainable, not stuck.
- **GBL reward path never lowers a legendary's floor** (3-0 + 2-1): during
  Battle Weekend floor-removal events the IV floor is removed for all reward
  encounters EXCEPT Tier-5 raid bosses; legendary GBL rewards keep the
  raid-style floor 10 (Niantic-confirmed pattern per Silph Road).
- **Ordinary wild-line ML species** (Gyarados, Dragonite lines, Togekiss,
  Sylveon, Goodra, Primarina, Florges, Gholdengo, Metagross, ...): wild floor
  is 0/0/0 (4/4/4 weather-boosted) but the species are grindable, so NOT
  limited; the published 12/12/12 evaluation floor stands.

## What the verification could NOT close (do not treat as settled)

1. **Shadow legendaries -- the largest gap, ~1/3 of the ML list.** No claim
   about shadow acquisition/floors survived the final adversarial merge
   (Shadow Palkia/Lugia/Metagross/Kyogre/Groudon/Mewtwo/Ho-Oh/Latios/Latias/
   Dialga/Garchomp/Hydreigon/Dragonite/Tyranitar/Annihilape/Reshiram/
   Gyarados). One 3-0-verified claim from the first-pass run (source:
   dittobase hundo-odds table; it did not survive into the final merged
   findings, provenance noted here for honesty) gave the shadow floor table
   as: **Giovanni encounters 6/6/6**, Shadow Raids with IV boost 7/7/7,
   grunt/leader shadows 0/0/0 (4/4/4 weather-boosted) -- i.e. NOT the 1/1/1
   folklore number. If Giovanni shadow legendaries are one-per-rotation and
   untradeable-while-shadow, a shadow-legendary ML guide arguably needs a
   **6/6/6 floor**, which no current guide has. Needs its own verification
   pass + Michael's judgment on whether rotation recurrence counts as
   "grindable."
2. **Tradeability of raid legendaries** (Necrozma, Kyogre, Lugia, Hero
   Zacian/Zamazenta, ...). If regular trades apply, tradeable raid legendaries
   have a sub-10 true minimum. The dedicated tradeability claims were refuted
   1-2 each; Bulbapedia's Trade page reportedly lists Crowned forms as
   trade-ineligible but only as side evidence.
3. **The generic floor table itself.** As STANDALONE claims, "raid/research/
   egg floor is 10/10/10" was refuted 1-2 and "regular trades floor 0/0/0
   rising by friendship (1/1/1 ... 5/5/5)" was refuted 0-3 in the final
   merge (refutation reasons not recorded; an earlier-pass dittobase claim
   for research floors was 3-0). The floor-10 NUMBERS above rest on the
   decision rule's stated assumptions plus corroborating side evidence
   (e.g. Bulbapedia's Special Research floor-10 note), not on an
   independently verified master table. Re-check before publishing any
   TRADE-derived floor.
4. **Not individually researched:** Tapu Lele, Landorus (Therian), Kyurem
   Black/White (fusion side evidence only), Origin Dialga/Palkia (raid debut
   at GO Tour Sinnoh 2024 verified 3-0 in the first pass; re-obtainability
   cadence not assessed in the final merge).
