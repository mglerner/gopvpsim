## Verified verdicts (3-0 votes unless noted)

### Melmetal: NOT limited -- default 12/12/12 treatment is correct (high)

The Mystery Box is repeatable indefinitely (3-day cooldown, re-armed by
sending any mon from GO to Let's Go or HOME -- free HOME tier suffices), and
Meltan appears as WILD encounters (floor 0/0/0, 4/4/4 weather-boosted).
Grindable, therefore not "stuck"; the 400-candy evolution itself presupposes
grinding. Primary source: Niantic Help Center (Mystery Box FAQ); Serebii +
Bulbapedia corroborate. If Melmetal was on anyone's candidate list for
`FLOOR_10_SPECIES`, take it off.

### New-species verdicts: nothing verifiably needs adding

- **Zacian (Crowned Sword) / Zamazenta (Crowned Shield)** (medium): the
  Crowned form is a TRANSFORMATION of a raid-caught Hero individual --
  Crowned IVs = the raid catch's IVs (raid floor 10). First change costs
  1,000 Crowned energy; after that, form swaps are free and unlimited
  (Niantic primary source). Crowned raids have already recurred (GO Fest 2025
  Global; Raid Hour Aug 2025), so re-obtainable when in rotation.
  Event-gated, not permanently stuck.
- **Necrozma (Dusk Mane / Dawn Wings)** (medium, 2-1 vote): fusion inherits
  BASE Necrozma's IVs exactly; Solgaleo/Lunala IVs are irrelevant. Fused
  floor = base Necrozma's floor (raids, recurring + GBL rank-20 pool). Side
  evidence (uncorroborated) reports the same inheritance rule for Kyurem
  Black/White fusions.
- **Solgaleo / Lunala** (medium): NOT capped at the one-time Cosmog research
  line -- both had 5-star raid debuts July 2025 and sat in the Season-23 GBL
  rank-20 reward pool. Re-obtainable, not stuck.
- **GBL reward path never lowers a legendary's floor** (3-0 + 2-1): during
  Battle Weekend floor-removal events the IV floor is removed for all reward
  encounters EXCEPT Tier-5 raid bosses; legendary GBL rewards keep the
  raid-style floor 10 (Niantic-confirmed pattern per Silph Road).
- **Ordinary wild-line ML species** (Gyarados, Dragonite lines, Togekiss,
  Sylveon, Goodra, Primarina, Florges, Gholdengo, Metagross, ...): wild floor
  is 0/0/0 (4/4/4 weather-boosted) but the species are grindable, so NOT
  limited; the published 12/12/12 evaluation floor stands.

## Shadow-legendary follow-up — RESOLVED 2026-07-03

The open-question #3 above (shadow legendaries, ~1/3 of the ML top-60) was the
one real candidate gap. Commissioned a focused adversarially-verified deep
research pass (103 agents, 8 findings). **Bottom line: none of the 17 ML shadow
legendaries needs a floor below 12/12/12; no code change.**

Two independent legs, both against the folklore:

1. **The floor NUMBER dispute is settled: the "1/1/1 shadow floor" is folklore
   and applies to none of these.** Giovanni shadows are guaranteed **6/6/6**
   (every stat >= 6, since July 2020; high, 3-0, Bulbapedia + GO Hub). Shadow
   Raid catches floor at **6/6/6** (->~7/7/7 with the event IV-boost research;
   high, 3-0, Bulbapedia + FleeceKing datamine). Only grunt/leader wild shadows
   drop to 0/0/0 (4/4/4 weather-boosted) — irrelevant to legendaries.

2. **Grindability: none is genuinely one-shot, so "stuck at the floor" never
   applies.** Six of the 17 are confirmed repeatable 5-star Shadow-Raid bosses
   (Palkia, Kyogre, Groudon, Mewtwo, Ho-Oh, Latios; high, 3-0) — raid as many as
   you find, keep the best. The seven shadow pseudo-legendaries (Metagross,
   Garchomp, Hydreigon, Dragonite, Tyranitar, Annihilape, Gyarados) come from
   grunts/leaders/lower raids and are plainly grindable. Giovanni legendaries
   are obtainable more than once per rotation (GO Pass Deluxe = 2 Super Rocket
   Radars; a radar-stacking loophole gives free players many).

**Confidence split (never-ship-unflagged honesty):** the verdict is HIGH for the
~13 species that are clearly grindable (Shadow-Raid legendaries + pseudo-legendaries).
It is MEDIUM for the ~4 Giovanni-PRIMARY legendaries with no confirmed Shadow-Raid
window (Dialga, Latias, Lugia, Reshiram), because their grindability leans on the
**weakest load-bearing finding** — multi-copy-per-Giovanni-rotation (2-1;
partly relies on paid GO Pass Deluxe + a loophole Niantic could patch). Even in
the worst case for those four (loophole patched AND truly one-per-rotation), the
correct floor would be a bounded, known **6/6/6 — never the 1/1/1 folklore**, and
6/6/6 is only ~1 damage tier of headroom below the 12/12/12 default.

**Recommendation:** keep 12/12/12 for all 17 (no change). OPTIONAL belt-and-
suspenders if Michael wants zero exposure: evaluate Dialga/Latias/Lugia/Reshiram
(Shadow) down to 6/6/6 in their ML guides — a small, bounded widening for the
four whose grindability is medium-confidence. Re-check acquisition when events
rotate (grindability is event-dependent; e.g. Groudon went Giovanni-only ->
Shadow Raids Sept 2025).

This CLOSES the limited-availability IV-floor investigation: no `FLOOR_10_SPECIES`
change, Melmetal explicitly not limited, and the shadow gap resolved with a
no-change verdict (+ one optional bounded widening for Michael's call).
