Why the rankings differ

The reference and our simulator optimize for different things:

  • Reference approach: Identifies specific matchup-flipping thresholds (bulkpoints/breakpoints against named meta threats) and recommends IVs that hit the most thresholds. Favors defense-heavy spreads.
  • Our approach: Ranks by average battle score across the entire Championship Series meta (39 opponents). Our top IVs sacrifice defense for HP, which is better on average but may miss specific matchup flips.

Both approaches are valid — they answer different questions. A complete analysis should present both.

Azumarill divergence

The reference claims def > 143.03 gives a bulkpoint vs rank #1 Azumarill. Our analysis:

  • Azumarill rank #1 (0/15/15 L40.0) has atk = 88.51
  • Bubble damage: 3 across all tested defense values (no break in 140–145 range)
  • Ice Beam damage: 23 across all tested values
  • Play Rough damage: 28 at def 143.03, drops to 27 at def 144.00

No per-hit damage change at def = 143.03. The 1-2s matchup flip may be a battle-level accumulation effect or the threshold may be entangled with HP. Needs investigation with all 9 shield scenarios.
