Why defense matters more with all 9 scenarios

  • Shield-disadvantage scenarios (0v1, 0v2, 1v2) punish low-def spreads that can't survive unshielded charged moves
  • HP-only spreads drop because damage reduction from defense is multiplicative while HP is additive
  • The reference's threshold-based approach handles this naturally — it identifies matchup flips regardless of scenario weighting
