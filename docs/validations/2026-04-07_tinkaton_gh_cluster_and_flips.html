4. The Steelix Problem

Nearly every IV spread's flip profile is dominated by Steelix. Low-HP spreads (HP ≤ 140) lose 5–7 Steelix matchups. HP ≥ 141 reduces to 1–2. HP ≥ 142 with adequate defense eliminates Steelix losses entirely.

GH Great threshold problem

The "GH Great" threshold (def ≥ 143.03, HP ≥ 138) includes low-HP spreads that have net negative flip profiles:
