here is a write-up i did for spidops iv spreads. i trimmed it down to only include matchups that felt relevant/important. please enjoy 👍

## spidops

### attack
* 109.82 attack nets a breakpoint vs rank #1 cradily which flips the 0s

* 110.24 attack picks up the rank #2 golisopod in the 0s. 111.58 attack picks up the rank #1 golisopod (the iv spread is 0/8/15 which skews towards hp, so the 0/12/12 rank #2 was also used for a more balanced high rank iv spread). any spidops IV spread always win the 0s vs default IV golisopod (4/15/13)

* 110.54 attack nets a breakpoint vs default IV galarian moltres (4/10/15) which flips the 2s

* 110.92 attack picks up florges in the 0s and 1-0s because you get a breakpoint before the first chilling water & you do one extra damage with shadow claw after 2 chilling waters

* 111.34 attack picks up the 1-2s vs rank #1 unovan stunfisk by increasing lunge damage to KO stunfisk after four of them

* 112.03 attack picks up default iv togekiss in the 0s with additional rock tomb damage. 113.14 attack is needed for the rank #1 togekiss

* 112.46 attack nets a breakpoint vs default IV shadow annihilape (4/13/13) which flips the 0s. 113.57 attack covers the rank #1

* 113.03 attack nets a breakpoint vs rank #2 forretress (as opposed to rank #1, which is hp-skewed) to flip the 1-0s and 2-1s

### bulk
anything here can work with more defense/less hp or vice versa. if you have something close to any of these stats, its probably worth looking at the sims to check

* 135.74 defense with 130+ hp flips the 2-1s vs the rank #1 furret. 137.51 defense with 130+ hp covers the default IV furret (4/14/13)

* 137.51 defense with 132+ hp flips the 1-2s vs the rank #1 tinkaton. 138.88 defense with 132+ hp covers the default IV tinkaton (4/15/14)

* 138.09 defense with 132+ hp covers the rank #1 shadow kanto marowak in the 1-2s. you need near-max hp to beat any attack weighted IV spreads

* 138.88 defense with 132+ hp covers the default IV shadow talonflame (4/12/15) in the 2-1s. this is very IV dependent for both pokemon

* 139.94 defense with 133+ hp flips the 1s vs the rank #1 blastoise. its possible to flip against higher attack blastoise with more defense/hp as long as you live two hydro cannons

* 140.67 defense with 132+ hp flips the 1s vs the rank #1 altaria without baits by reducing sky attack damage. 140.85 defense with 133+ hp covers the default IV altaria (4/12/13)

### summary

* a slight attack-weighted spidops seems really strong, but a bulk-focus spidops also looks solid

* bulk-focused stats i recommend: 109.82 attack, 138.88 defense minimum (140.67 is preferred), and as much hp as possible. 2/15/15 is pretty cool

* slight attack weight stats i recommend: 113.03 attack, 138.88 defense, and as much hp as possible. 1/7/1 is pretty cool

### Note

spidops is less meta relevant now that it was when this deep dive was done.
